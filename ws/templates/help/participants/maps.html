Trail Maps

Useful Online Resources

Useful online resources for making trail maps and analyzing trails:

  • Caltopo: Currently considered one of the best all-around mappers for the continental United States. Its expanding set of features also allows for weather forecasting and PDF printouts great for groups.
  • Hill Map: Trails overlaid onto Google Maps. Distances not always accurate.
  • ACME Mapper: A very fun and useful webpage
  • AMC Trip Planner: (Subscription) is a paid but excellent resource for planning trips specifically in the White Mountains. It provides detailed distance, maps, and trail conditions and allows for the creation of custom itineraries.
