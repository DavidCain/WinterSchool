Renting a Car

Renting a Car Through MIT

MIT students, faculty, and staff who wish to rent a vehicle for a MITOC trip are eligible to use MIT's corporate agreements with Budget and Avis for business travelers. In addition to offering discounted rates and waiving the requirement for renters to be at least 25 years of age, the rental agency provides insurance coverage against both liability and physical damage. The trip must have been approved by the club and registered with the MIT Student Activities Office; contact mitoc-bursar@mit.edu to obtain confirmation.

Visit the link for either rental agency and click "Make a Business Rental". Make sure to decline liability and collision/physical damage coverage when reserving or picking up the vehicle. More details are available from the MIT Office of Insurance.

Car Sharing Services

If using a car sharing service such as Zipcar, be aware that the mileage included in the hourly/daily rate is often less than the round-trip distance to and from out-of-state trailheads frequently visited by MITOC, which may result in additional charges.
